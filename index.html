Dev Churiwala

I am a final year undergraduate pursuing a double major in biology and computer science at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani. I work on machine learning problems and blockchain technologies.

Currently, I am pursuing my undergraduate thesis at the Autonomous Networks Research Group at the University of Southern California under Dr. Bhaskar Krishnamachari. Our work proposes a novel Q-learning approach to maximizing fees on automated market-making protocols.

I am also the co-founder of Liquibrium, a stablecoin exchange on the Tezos blockchain. Liquibrium is designed for extremely efficient stablecoin trading with low-risk supplemental income for liquidity providers.

Prior to this, I worked with Dr. Kristofer Bouchard at the University of California, Berkeley. Our work focused on applying machine learning to learn complex representations of proteins to predict protein function.

My first name is "Dev" and informally I go by the nickname "DC".
